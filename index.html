About Erranova SECDEF

Erranova SECDEF (formerly Terranova SECDEF) is a NATO-aligned cybersecurity firm specialising in sovereign defence solutions for governments, allied military forces, and critical infrastructure operators.

Frequently Asked Questions

What classification levels does Erranova support? ▼

Erranova SECDEF supports security operations up to SECRET level across NATO-allied nations. Our sovereign cloud infrastructure is designed for ITAR, EAR, and national classification requirements with full data residency compliance.

Do your analysts hold security clearances? ▼

Yes. All SOC analysts and incident responders hold active security clearances appropriate to the engagement level, up to and including Top Secret/SCI eligibility for applicable programmes.

How quickly can Erranova deploy to a new environment? ▼

Standard deployment takes 2 to 4 weeks depending on scope and classification requirements. Emergency incident response teams can be activated within 15 minutes of engagement.

What compliance frameworks do you support? ▼

We align with ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, CMMC, NIST 800-53, NIST 800-171, NERC CIP, IEC 62443, FedRAMP, and NATO-specific security standards including STANAG requirements.

Can Erranova integrate with existing security tools? ▼

Absolutely. Our platform integrates with all major SIEM, SOAR, EDR, and ITSM tools. We also support custom API integrations and have pre-built connectors for over 200 security products.
